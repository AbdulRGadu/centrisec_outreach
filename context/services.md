# Centrisec — Services Catalog

Phrased as outcomes. Use these to ground what Centrisec can actually do for a prospect.
Never list more than one or two services in a cold email — pick what fits their situation.

## Cybersecurity & IT Services

- **User Security** — reduce risk from human behaviour: security awareness training,
  phishing simulation, identity & access management, insider threat protection, privileged
  access security, user risk monitoring. Outcome: fewer people-driven incidents and proof
  of who is actually at risk.
- **App & Database Security** — protect business applications, APIs, and databases from
  exposure and misuse: security reviews, API assessment, database hardening, secure
  development support. Outcome: the systems that hold sensitive data stop being the soft spot.
- **Endpoint Security** — laptops, desktops, servers, and devices hardened and visible:
  protection advisory, configuration hardening, endpoint risk visibility, response support.
- **Cloud Security** — secure cloud environments, workloads, identities, and configurations:
  posture review, access review, configuration hardening, monitoring support.
- **Network Security** — protect the infrastructure connecting users, systems, and branches:
  security review, firewall and segmentation advisory, secure access design, exposure reduction.

## Managed Security & Support

- **VAPT (Vulnerability Assessment & Penetration Testing)** — discover vulnerabilities,
  validate real exposure, prioritise fixes, and produce technical audit evidence. Outcome:
  you know what an attacker could actually do, and you have proof the gaps were closed.
- **Managed SOC** — 24/7 monitoring of endpoints and cloud, log consolidation, suspicious
  behaviour alerts, posture reporting. Outcome: someone is actually watching.
- **MDR (Managed Detection & Response)** — detect, investigate, contain: endpoint detection
  and response, rapid containment, threat hunting, incident analysis. Outcome: shorter
  attacker dwell time, smaller business impact.
- **Cyber Threat Intelligence** — dark web exposure monitoring, sector-specific threat
  alerts, indicator checks. Outcome: decisions guided by threats that are actually relevant.
- **WAAP (Web Application & API Protection)** — protect public-facing apps and APIs:
  OWASP Top 10 shielding, bot detection, DDoS mitigation support, continuous edge rules.
- **Identity Management as a Service** — SSO architecture, MFA deployment, user lifecycle
  automation, privileged directory reviews. Outcome: the right people have the right access,
  and nothing more.

## Compliance Readiness

- **ISO 27001 readiness** and **NDPR/NDPA compliance support** — gap assessment, control
  implementation support, evidence collection, and audit preparation. Outcome: audits and
  regulator reviews stop being chaotic scrambles; leadership gets documentation it can
  stand behind.

## Consultancy, Development & IT Infrastructure

- **Strategic IT consulting** — help leadership make better decisions on architecture,
  infrastructure, security, and digital operations.
- **IT infrastructure automation & management**, **enterprise software development**
  (secure portals, dashboards, APIs, internal platforms), and **implementation services**.
