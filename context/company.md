## Who we are

Centrisec (centrisec.com) is a fully-managed cybersecurity firm based in Lagos, Nigeria,
serving organisations across Nigeria and West Africa.

Tagline: **"Security should not be complicated."**

We help organisations identify and reduce cybersecurity risks **before they become costly
incidents** from phishing and employee security awareness to protecting business systems,
sensitive data, and critical operations.

Our positioning has three pillars:

1. **Human** reduce risk from people and behaviour: awareness, phishing resistance,
   access misuse, insider exposure.
2. **Technical** validate that controls actually work: testing, monitoring, detection,
   response.
3. **Executive** turn security activity into evidence and reporting that leadership,
   auditors, and regulators respect.

## Why Centrisec (differentiators)

When drafting an email, weave in exactly ONE of these, chosen to fit the recipient
naturally, in plain words, never as a boast:

1. **Before, not after.** We find and reduce risk before it becomes a costly incident,
   instead of selling cleanup after the damage is done.
2. **Fully managed, end to end.** Clients do not need an in-house security team or deep
   technical expertise we run the programme and they see the results.
3. **Evidence leadership and auditors respect.** Every engagement produces plain-language,
   audit-ready reporting. Executives understand their risk without decoding technical noise.
4. **Local regulatory depth.** We work inside the Nigerian and West African regulatory
   reality every day NDPA/NDPR compliance, ISO 27001 readiness, and what local auditors
   and regulators actually ask for.
5. **We look where others don't.** Most providers focus on the external perimeter. Breaches
   usually start with people, excessive access, weak authentication, and missing response
   processes that is where we start.
6. **Measurable outcomes over jargon.** Security you can see moving: user risk scores,
   validated fixes, closed gaps not shelfware reports.

## Proof assets

- **Security readiness checklist** a short, practical checklist that helps an organisation
  spot common security gaps without a complex technical assessment. Offered in cold emails;
  sent when the prospect replies. Never attached to a cold email, never linked.
- A short **proposal** tailored to the prospect's security priorities  offered as the
  call-to-action; written and sent manually after a positive reply.

## What we believe (brand voice anchors)

- Security should be measurable.
- Compliance preparation should not be chaotic.
- Leaders should understand risk without needing to decode technical noise.
- People are where breaches start showing who is actually at risk matters more than
  another appliance.

## Tone rules (hard requirements for anything written in Centrisec's name)

- Calm, plain-spoken, straightforward sentences. No jargon, no acronyms without need.
- Confident without superlatives. Never "the best", "world-class", "cutting-edge".
- Never fear-monger. No breach statistics used as scare tactics, no "hackers are coming".
- Never invent facts about the recipient, their company, or their systems. If we don't
  know something, speak in terms of what is typical for organisations like theirs.
- Never overpromise. We reduce and manage risk; we do not "guarantee" or "eliminate" it.
- Write like a helpful professional, not a marketer.
